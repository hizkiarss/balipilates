What You Will Learn

Our curriculum is centred around Pilates principles that are scientifically grounded and practically focused.

By the end of this training, you will be able to:

  • Demonstrate a strong understanding of Pilates principles, philosophy, and methodology
  • Apply functional anatomy and biomechanics to movement analysis and instruction
  • Confidently perform and teach both classical repertoire and contemporary variations
  • Design structured, effective programs for private sessions and group classes
  • Teach mixed-level clients with appropriate modifications, regressions, and progressions
  • Deliver clear, confident instruction using effective verbal, tactile, and visual cueing
  • Integrate studio apparatus and props safely and effectively
  • Apply professional standards, ethics, and client communication in a studio environment
  • Build confidence through real teaching practice and apprenticeship experience
  • Develop the skills and presence required to begin teaching professionally

While many students choose to complete their hours in Bali — as the studio is available for 6 months free of charge as part of your registration — the training, including practice and teaching hours, can be completed in your home country. We provide international students with an EYT Apprentice referral letter that you can take to a studio in your home country to request either renting their equipment or applying for an apprentice teaching role to complete your hours.
