Your Path

Choose Your Certification Path

Two pathways offering diverse certification options depending on your career goals and prior Pilates knowledge.

Foundation

Mat & Reformer Teacher Certification

Build teaching skills using mat-work coaching and develop a lasting appreciation of Pilates foundations.

  • Approx. 300 hrs of full-time training
  • Combination of in-person seminars and online study
  • Develop strong teaching foundations and confidence
  • Perfect for aspiring Pilates teachers and enthusiasts
Apply – Foundation
Comprehensive

Comprehensive Teacher Certification

This programme covers everything from mat to all apparatus, including barrel, trapeze, and private sessions.

  • Includes Cadillac, Chair & Combo training
  • Approx. 600 hrs of additional study
  • Final assessment and exam practice potential
  • Ideal for serious teachers, studios, and private clients
Apply – Comprehensive
Curriculum

What You Will Learn

Our curriculum is centred around Pilates principles that are scientifically grounded and practically focused.

Understand the human body from the inside out. Explore how muscles, joints, and fascia interact during Pilates movement, giving you the knowledge to cue effectively and keep clients safe.
Develop your unique teaching voice. Learn how to give clear, effective cues — both verbal and tactile — and how to read a room, adapt sessions on the fly, and build real rapport with clients.
Hours of supervised practice teaching across mat and equipment, with structured feedback from mentors and peer groups to fast-track your growth and confidence.
Learn to create customised programmes for diverse clients — beginners, athletes, post-natal, and rehabilitation — with well-structured progressions and modifications.
From setting up your first private client to managing a studio schedule, gain the business acumen to build a sustainable, fulfilling teaching career.
